Oreo Frappe

Our Oreo Frappe is a fan favorite for anyone underneath the drinking age or a sweet tooth. It offers a perfect blend of creamy indulgence and the classic chocolatey goodness of Oreo cookies. Here's what it's made from:

-1 cup of chilled milk
-2 cups of ice
-2-3 Oreo cookies
-2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup
-2 tablespoons of sweetened whipped cream
-1/2 teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
-2-3 tablespoons of sugar

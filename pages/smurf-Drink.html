Smurf Drink

Our Smurf Drink is a quirky and eye-catching beverage that is as tasty as it is enjoyable. This blue-colored design is ideal for people who want to add a bit of enchantment to their night out. It's made of the following ingredients:

-1 oz Blue Curacao liqueur
-1 oz coconut rum
-1 oz pineapple juice
-1 oz cream of coconut
-1/2 oz lemon juice
-Blue Colored Syrup
